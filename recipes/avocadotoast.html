Avocado, Feta, and Mint on Sourdough Toast

Description

This is a recipe I've eaten weekly for the past ten years. No, seriously, I'm that obsessed with it. I can easily make it without measurements. Consisting of only five ingredients, this healthy avocado toast can be whipped up in a jiffy, and makes a surprisingly filling breakfast or lunch. Sourdough bread is suggested, but a whole wheat or sprouted grain bread tastes great too. Other than that, no substitutions allowed!

Recipe from Food52

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta
  • 4 slices crusty sourdough bread

Steps

  1. Use a fork to smash the avocado into paste. Add the lemon, salt, and mint and mix.
  2. Stir in the feta, then adjust seasoning to taste.
  3. Toast bread and spread avocado mixture onto it.

That's it!
